<p class="c13"><span class="c1">Rep. David Trone, a Democrat, has served as the U.S. Representative for
    Maryland&#x2019;s 6th Congressional District since 2019, representing all or part of Montgomery, Frederick,
    Garrett, Allegany and Washington counties.</span></p>
<p class="c13"><span class="c1">After losing his 24-year-old nephew to a fentanyl overdose in 2016, he focused his
    2018 campaign heavily on combating the opioid epidemic, outlining a $100-billion action plan to address the
    crisis.</span></p>
<p class="c13"><span class="c1">Trone has advocated for criminal justice reform, equal pay for women,
    community-based policing, raising the minimum wage and protecting the environment since 2018.</span></p>
