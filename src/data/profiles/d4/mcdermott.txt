<h1 class="c18" id="h.llrnlykla4n8"><span>4th Congressional District</span></h1>
<h3 class="c11" id="h.40lvharwa89i"><span class="c7">George McDermott (R)</span></h3>
<p class="c9"><span>George McDermott&#x2019;s </span><span class="c4 c19"><a class="c2" href="http://www.georgemcdermott.com/youdontknow.html">campaign
      website</a></span><span class="c1">&#xA0;is devoted to uncovering &#x201C;judicial terrorism.&#x201D;</span></p>
<p class="c8"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<p class="c9"><span>In his words, government officials are not being held accountable to the Constitution. He
    describes himself as a victim&#x2019;s rights advocate for victims of white collar crime and &#x201C;judicial
    abuse&#x201D; in particular. McDermott writes that he was </span><span class="c4 c19"><a class="c2" href="https://redmaryland.com/2020/01/red-maryland-candidate-survey-george-mcdermott-for-congress-district-4/">inspired
      to study law after losing his businesses</a></span><span class="c1">&#xA0;in a court battle in the
    1980s.</span></p>
<p class="c8"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">This will be McDermott&#x2019;s third time running against Rep. Anthony Brown for U.S.
    Representative for Maryland&#x2019;s 4th Congressional District &#x2014; he lost by a margin of more than 50
    percentage points in both previous races.</span></p>
<p class="c8"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">McDermott has said he never has and never will endorse President Donald Trump,
    according to The Capital Gazette. </span></p>
<p class="c8"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">Here&#x2019;s where he stands on the issues:</span></p>
<p class="c8"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<ul class="c17 lst-kix_fibill7db7ls-0 start">
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">McDermott believes income inequality is a salient issue in the U.S. He disapproves
      of Trump&#x2019;s 2017 federal tax cuts. </span></li>
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">McDermott supports a federal face-covering mandate. He believes more funding
      should go toward private citizens and state and local governments in Congress&#x2019;s next economic relief
      package.</span></li>
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">McDermott has said he supports legislation calling for greater transparency from
      law enforcement and more accountability from police officers. </span></li>
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">McDermott is wary of an impending climate crisis in the U.S. and said he views the
      actions proposed in the Green New Deal as a start in dealing with this issue. He has indicated support for
      eliminating fossil fuel dependency and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.</span></li>
</ul>
