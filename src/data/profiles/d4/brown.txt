<p class="c9"><span class="c1">Rep. Anthony Brown is seeking his third term as representative for Maryland&#x2019;s
    4th Congressional District. Brown first entered politics in 1998 as a state lawmaker representing Prince
    George&#x2019;s County. In 2006, he was elected lieutenant governor under former Gov. Martin O&#x2019;Malley. He
    ran for governor in 2014 and lost to current Gov. Larry Hogan. </span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">Brown is a retired colonel in the U.S. Army Reserves.</span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">In the past, Brown has advocated for increased investments in community colleges and
    reduced public university tuition. As lieutenant governor, Brown worked to expand access to health care and
    increase public-private partnerships for infrastructure projects such as the Purple Line.</span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">Here&#x2019;s where he stands on the issues:</span></p>
<ul class="c17 lst-kix_oq25b9iqt8tn-0 start">
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">Brown supports tax incentives for renewable energy and the restoration of the
      Clean Water Act. He backs former President Barack Obama&#x2019;s Climate Action Plan to reduce carbon
      emissions. </span></li>
</ul>
<p class="c8 c16"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<ul class="c17 lst-kix_oq25b9iqt8tn-0">
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">Brown supports raising the minimum wage. He advocates for bringing the FBI to
      Prince George&#x2019;s County to create more private sector jobs in security and law enforcement. Brown wants
      to provide tax relief to small businesses and invest more in minority and women-owned businesses.</span></li>
</ul>
<p class="c8 c16"><span class="c1"></span></p>
<ul class="c17 lst-kix_oq25b9iqt8tn-0">
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">Brown voted for the Heroes Act, a COVID-19 economic stimulus bill drafted by
      congressional Democrats. Recently, he introduced the Health Enterprise Zones Act, which seeks to give minority
      and low-income communities greater access to quality health care. </span></li>
</ul>
<p class="c8"><span class="c24 c23"></span></p>
<ul class="c17 lst-kix_oq25b9iqt8tn-0">
  <li class="c3"><span class="c1">Brown supports the Justice in Policing Act, which aims to prevent police abuse. He
      supports the creation of a federal database requiring state and local law enforcement to report deaths
      involving police officers. Brown has advocated for a national truth and reconciliation commission to examine
      institutional racism.</span></li>
</ul>
