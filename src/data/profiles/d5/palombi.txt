<p class="c9"><span class="c1">Chris Palombi is a Calvert County native and former U.S. Capitol Police Officer
    running for office for the first time. </span></p>
<p class="c9"><span class="c1">He served on the Capitol Police force for nearly five years out of college and was
    detailed in both the House and Senate chambers. Palombi then pursued a career in web development and became
    webmaster of St. Mary&apos;s Ryken High School in Leonardtown. He coaches ice hockey at the school and served as
    president of the Maryland Student Hockey League from 2013 to 2015. </span></p>
<p class="c9"><span>The candidate says he got into politics after witnessing unproductive </span><span class="c4 c19"><a class="c2" href="https://www.thebaynet.com/articles/0120/chris-palombi-announces-campaign-to-unseat-steny-hoyer.html">divisiveness</a></span><span class="c1">&#xA0;on Capitol Hill. Palombi touts traditional conservative beliefs such as limited government and
    fiscal responsibility. </span></p>
