<p class="c13"><span class="c1">Takoma Park resident Jamie Raskin has served two terms in the House since being
    elected in 2016. He is a returning member of the House Judiciary Committee, the Committee on Oversight and
    Reform and the Committee on House Administration.</span></p>
<p class="c13"><span class="c1">Before being elected to Congress, Raskin served three terms in the state senate and
    taught constitutional law at American University for over 25 years, according to his campaign website.</span>
</p>
